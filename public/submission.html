Blog Submission Guidelines:



Thank you for your interest in contributing to our blog! To ensure a smooth submission process and maintain the quality of our content, please adhere to the following guidelines:

Relevance: Submissions should be relevant to our blog's theme and audience. We cover topics related to creative topics related to social issues.

Originality: We prioritize original content that has not been published elsewhere. Plagiarized or heavily duplicated content will not be accepted.

Quality: Articles should be well-written, informative, and engaging. Aim for a minimum of 900 words. Proper grammar, punctuation, and coherence are essential.

Formatting: Please submit your article in a Google Doc or Word document format. Use clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points where appropriate to enhance readability.

Images: If your article includes images, ensure they are high-quality, relevant, and have appropriate permissions for use. Include image files as separate attachments with clear captions or embed links to images hosted on reputable sites.

Links: Limit self-promotional links to a minimum. All links included in the article should be relevant and add value to the content. We reserve the right to remove or modify any links that are deemed irrelevant or promotional.

Author Bio: Include a brief author bio (approximately 50 words) at the end of your article. You may include a link to your personal website or social media profiles.

Editing: Our editorial team may edit submissions for clarity, style, and adherence to our guidelines. We will notify you of any significant changes made.

Submission Process: Send your article as an email attachment to craftuby.abc@gmail.com with the subject line "Blog Submission: [Your Article Title]". In the body of the email, provide a brief summary or introduction of your article.

Response Time: We strive to review submissions promptly and will notify you of the status of your submission within a week.

Rights: By submitting an article, you grant us the right to edit and publish the content on our blog. Once published, the content may not be reproduced elsewhere without permission. Thank you for considering contributing to our blog. We look forward to reviewing your submission! If you have any questions about these guidelines or the submission process, please feel free to contact us.
